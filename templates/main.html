About our Cafeteria

The Cafeteria "Aurora" is open daily from 7 a.m. to midnight.

Located on the first floor, the Cafeteria "Aurora" offers a selection of hot and cold foods, soups and a salad bar, as well as a wide variety of beverages and desserts. Full meal service is available during the following hours:

Breakfast: 7 a.m. to 10 a.m.

Lunch: 11 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.

Dinner: 4:30 p.m. to 6:30 p.m
